About the Hedge

What is the Hedge Buffer?

Gold and currency prices fluctuate constantly. The Precautionary Zakat adds a buffer percentage to your total wealth before calculating the 2.5% Zakat obligation.

This ensures you pay Zakat on a slightly inflated estimate, providing peace of mind that your full obligation is covered even if prices change during the year.

Formula

Hedge Zakat = Total Wealth × (1 + Buffer%) × 2.5%

About Nisab

What is Nisab?

The Nisab is the minimum threshold of wealth that makes Zakat obligatory. It is equivalent to 85 grams of pure gold (24K).

If your total wealth is below the Nisab, no Zakat is due. Zakat becomes obligatory after holding wealth above the Nisab for a full lunar year (Hawl).

This calculator does not track Hawl (one-year period). Please consult a scholar for full Zakat guidance.
